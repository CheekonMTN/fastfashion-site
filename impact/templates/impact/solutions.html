Solutions

Individuals aren’t responsible for fixing the fashion system—but we can still take actions that matter. Here are realistic steps, plus a brief fairness and ethics check to keep things grounded.

Fairness & Ethics

Sustainability often costs more. Not everyone can afford slow fashion, sustainable brands, or specialty materials. Any “solution” must respect budgets, needs, and access.

Realistic Actions

  • Buy fewer new items and wear the ones you have longer.
  • When affordable, try second-hand or clothing swaps.
  • Repair small rips or missing buttons instead of replacing clothes.
  • Donate or recycle textiles locally when possible.
  • Talk with friends about habits and sustainable options.
